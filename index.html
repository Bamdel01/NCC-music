WORSHIP SERVICE

In our worship service at New Covenant Church Ijokodo Centre; usually, you will find: the young adults, parents, elderly, and the aged having a sweet communion. In this big family, what we share in common is love. The young continuously having an opportunity to learn from the wisdom and accumulated experience of the old; and the old being inspired by the strength and unique ability of the young. This service starts by 9am and ends at exactly 11am

THE YOUTH CHURCH

Here at New Covenant Church Ijokodo Center, the youth service is for young people basically and all those who are youth at heart. In this service, expect: High praise, dance/choreography, poetry, drama, several varieties of soul lifting ministrations, etc. Futhermore, you get to hear the undiluted word of God presented to you in a contemporary way and to pour out your heart to the Lord. This service is anchored by the young through the help of the Holy Spirit. We hold the youth service from 7am – 8:30am every Sunday.

THE TEENAGE CHURCH

Fellowshipping in the teenage Church are our children in the secondary school. The secondary school comprise of both Junior (JSS) and Senior (SSS) categories respectively. So, during the worship service which holds between 9am and 11am, our teenagers enjoy undiluted fellowship under the watch and guide of Holy Spirit-filled teachers and mentors who they can relate to. Our teenagers engage in other varieties of activities like: Games, Stage play, Trips, Discussions/Forums, etc.

CHILDREN CHURCH

Here at New Covenant Church Ijokodo Center, the youth service is for young people basically and all those who are youth at heart. In this service, expect: High praise, dance/choreography, poetry, drama, several varieties of soul lifting ministrations, etc. Futhermore, you get to hear the undiluted word of God presented to you in a contemporary way and to pour out your heart to the Lord. This service is anchored by the young through the help of the Holy Spirit. We hold the youth service from 7am – 8:30am every Sunday.

COVENANT MEN INTERNATIONAL (CMI)

In New Covenant Church Ijokodo centre, we have an active network of men under the CMI. Futhermore, comprising the covenant men international are married men. They meet regularly in fellowship to discuss issues pertaining to the health and well being of each other, their homes, their businesses, careers, children, wives, the Church, economic and community development, national development and even their individual walk with the Lord. They are also the Fathers in the house, hence, they celebrate Father's Day on a dedicated Sunday in the year.

COVENANT WOMEN INTERNATIONAL

All the members of Covenant women international in New Covenant Church Ijokodo Center are married. They meet regularly to fellowship in prayers and to cater for each other’s welfare. They are also a active part of liberty where they hold an annual Outreach which involves Medical Outreach [which impacts significantly the health of many people within and outside the church.], Evangelism [A platform to win many souls to the Lord]. They are also involved in entrepreneurship and empowerment programs alike.
